About Us


Who We Are:

Breakdance Club is NYU's prime destination for anything related to this art form. Our purpose is to create a community around breaking that anyone, and everyone, can participate in. We conduct weekly lessons/independent practice, as well as performances at events hosted by other organizations. We'd love for you to stop by and get down with us!
