About Us

Where affordable apartments meet conscious construction

At Gantry Green,

The beloved LIC vibe meets modern luxury and the newest environmentally-conscious technology in a neo-industrial building on Fiftieth Avenue, just steps from the East River Waterfront. Conceived by Steffanie Schron, these 85 Hunters Point apartments are nestled in one of New York’s most desirable neighborhoods. From the rooftop deck to the corners of each individual studio, one, two, and three bedroom apartment, upscale details and the latest in eco-technology, all of which grace every apartment, to create an eclectic urban living environment you’ll be proud to call home while having little to no negative impact on the environment. In fact, Gantry Green is the first building of its kind to actually have a positive impact on the environment:the building itself is modeled after the veins of leaves, taking in carbon dioxide and emitting oxygen into the air for the public to breathe. Floor-to-ceiling windows generate solar energy, and white oak cabinetry & flooring takes in the carbon dioxide you emit, and puts out oxygen for you to breathe. Sleek, black floor tiles absorb your body heat, and convert it to thermal heat to warm all rooms. Energy-star certified appliances use the most efficient methods for high performance, from bidets that recycle water to wash your hands to ovens whose metal absorbs your body heat, and thermal energy goes towards heating your food.

Community areas are carefully furnished and arranged to facilitate socializing as well as privacy. Bicycle storage, in-home washers and dryers, and a fitness center bring another measure of convenience to residential life, as well as a childcare center and a greenhouse that residents can purchase food from, or grow their own. The rooftop deck offers peak relaxation with beautiful views of the city. And as a pet-friendly community, your dogs and cats are welcome to join you here.

Eco-Edge

When you choose to live in these LIC apartments, you’re treated to a wealth of enviable amenities that make things more convenient and enjoyable.

Greenhouse

Produce grown on premises available for residents to purchase. Residents can volunteer, or reserve their own space to grow plants of their choice. Resident's compost used for fertilizing the greenhouse garden.

Childcare

Staff is attuned to the needs of each child in their care. We have rigorous protocols designed to ensure that both children and parents get the maximum benefit and enjoyment. Our Child Care room offers a clean, safe environment filled with toys, puzzles, educational games and art supplies. Video games, tapes and child-friendly television are also available.

Non-Wasteful Waste

Refuse rooms on each floor have options for landfill, recycling and compost. Contributions are tracked through scanning your building ID to unlock & activate chutes. Can receive deductions on monthly bills depending on how much you compost and recycle.

Renewable Resources

Building is 100% powered by solar panels and wind turbines, including the pool, gym, clubhouse, and greenhouse. All appliances are Energy-Star Certified, and all lights are LED. All water is recycled.

Encouraging Sustainability

Bike room, courtyard, and deck encourage residents to enjoy the outdoors.

Superb Staff

Our staff is experienced not only in hospitality, but also envrionmental studies. It is our goal to help you enjoy your home without hurting the environment.
