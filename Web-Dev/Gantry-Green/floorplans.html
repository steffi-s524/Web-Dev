The cookie-cutter approach doesn’t have a place at Gantry Green. We offer 20 unique floor plans, giving you the opportunity to choose the studio, one, two, or three bedroom apartment that feels most like home. Gantry Green goes above and beyond the fine details you’d expect to find in upscale LIC apartment buildings to create an eclectic urban living experience. As you enter, you’re greeted by a flood of natural light in the open concept living area, thanks to floor-to-ceiling windows with an expansive view of the bustling Hunters Point neighborhood you love. White oak floors take you from room to room, where you’ll find generous walk-in closets and your own washer/dryer, even in studio layouts. The modern kitchens, complete with sophisticated Calacatta porcelain backsplash, paneled dishwashers, high-gloss cabinetry, and Caesarstone Blizzard white countertops, offer a subtle European flare with built-in ovens and cooktops. Liebherr refrigerators are sleek and spacious, making it easy to stay organized and efficient in the kitchen. While there is no shortage of upscale Hunters Point apartment buildings, you will be pressed to find another boutique residence like Gantry Green. Contact us to schedule your private tour.
